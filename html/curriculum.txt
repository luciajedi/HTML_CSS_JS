HTML, CSS, JS - Jedi Course
HTML Exercises
------------------------

Create a page with two sections. 
In the first one, enter your contact information and in the second one, explain your professional goals and current studies. 
Also list your top qualities, and at the top part of the document add a profile image.

Finally, create a language section using a table. For each language you must specify writing and oral skills level.

At the end of the document, add a button that makes the user go to top.
